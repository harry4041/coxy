Who am I?

By trade I'm a shift technician at a power station. By passion - I am a developer.

I am a 27 year old married man with two children and currently based in Cambridgeshire. I spend a lot of my time working on personal projects. These range from web pages to Unity games to even some more hands on, mechanical projects like fixing up motorbikes. Though I've never had a professional career working as a developer, that is what I am striving for. After all, they say if you love what you do you'll never work a day in your life.
